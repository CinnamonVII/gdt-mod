
1. Rival Studios & The Global Stock Market
- Dynamic Competitor Landscape: Compete with a roster of rival studios who dynamically release games, gain fans, and grow their valuations.
- Stock Market Investment: Buy and sell shares in rival studios in 10% increments. Keep an eye on successful releases to make profit.
- Passive Revenue: Earn weekly dividends proportional to your ownership percentage whenever your rivals release a successful title.

2. Subsidiary Management & Co-Development
- Hostile Takeovers: Once you acquire a 50% or greater controlling stake in any rival studio, they become your subsidiary! You can also found a brand-new subsidiary from scratch for $5,000,000.
- Direct Staff Control: Manage your subsidiary's workforce. Hire and fire staff across 5 different tiers (from 1-Star interns to 5-star employees), balancing a deeper workforce system to add more complexity.
- Assign Tasks: Direct your subsidiary to develop games by choosing their Topic, Genre, Game Size (Small to AAA), and Platforms.
- Specializations: Configure your subsidiary to specialize in General projects, DLCs, Co-Development, or Game Releases.
- Free Co-Development Support: Leverage your subsidiary to co-develop with your main studio, injecting massive Design and Technology points into your active projects for free based on their team quality.

3. The Franchise
- Register Franchises: Turn your best releases into franchises.
- Dynamic Fanbase Score: Replaced the legacy experience/fans system with a 0-100 fanbaseScore and a 5-tier system to track your franchise strength.
- Massive Expansion Options: Spin off your franchises into Prequels, Sequels, Remakes, Remasters, Reboots, Spinoffs, Expansions, or Bundles. Develop them internally or delegate them to your subsidiaries for faster but maybe sloppier results.
- Corporate Acquisitions: Buy listed competitor franchises.
- Licensing Proposals: Receive and negotiate licensing offers from external studios in a streamlined details modal window to secure extra cash without interrupting your workflow.
- Legendary Crossovers: Merge two of your Tier 5 franchises in a massive $3,000,000 Crossover Event to combine their fanbases and create record-breaking revenue!

4. Modular DLC System & The DLC Wizard
- Extend the Lifecycle: Keep your games relevant and profitable. You can release up to 5 DLCs for any game under 10 years old.
- The 7-Page DLC Wizard: Custom design and launch DLCs using an interactive, multi-step creation wizard. Custom design every aspect of your release:
  * Identity & Theme: Pick from 10 distinct themes (like Dark Story, Final Chapter, Retro, or Sci-Fi).
  * DLC Types: Select from 8 specialized formats (Story Expansion, Character Pack, Season Pass, Map Pack, etc.) each with unique cost multipliers, fan expectations, and base attachment rates.
  * Content Scales: Scale your project across 6 sizes (from a Tiny cosmetic add-on to a Standalone expansion) that define base costs, times, and price points.
  * Resource Allocation: Custom assign 100 points across 14 development facets (Story, Graphics, Online Features, Balance, etc.).
  * Pricing Control: Decide the price of your DLC, keeping in mind the optimal price point for its scale; pricing too high or low impacts reviews and sales.
  * Marketing Campaign: Choose from 8 campaign strategies (like Social Media, TV, Worldwide, or Beta Access) to boost your launch units.
  * Release Timing: Plan your release strategy around special windows (Holiday, Anniversary, Publisher Showcases) to maximize impact.
- Subsidiary Delegation: Save your main team's time and assign a subsidiary to develop the DLC for free over 18 weeks using the 'Always DLC' specialization.
- Dynamic Reviews & Revenue: Released DLCs receive score reviews based on quality, base-game synergy, price fairness, and bug counts, generating steady weekly revenue based on base game sales and attachment caps. Extremely successful DLCs (score > 8) also boost your franchise's fanbase score (+5)!

5. 'The Grid' Expansion: Streaming Platform & Media Production
- Launching "The Grid": Found your own global streaming network with an initial setup investment of $25,000,000, launching with 100,000 starting subscribers.
- Subscription Pricing: Customize monthly pricing options ($4.99, $7.99, $9.99, $12.99, $14.99, or $19.99/mo) to directly dictate your weekly revenue stream (paid out monthly).
- Server Infrastructure Upkeep: Rented servers support your user capacity in tiers of 1,000,000 subscribers per server.
  * Server Rent: Costs $150,000/week per server, scaling down by $500 for each server rented to a minimum of $50,000/week per server, plus a base flat platform fee of $5,000/week.
  * Overload Warnings: If subscribers exceed capacity, churn increases. Exceeding capacity for more than 4 consecutive weeks causes a server crash and a PR disaster, losing 90% of your subscriber base.
- Subscribers & Growth Dynamics:
  * Marketing Budget: Configure monthly marketing campaigns ($0, $50k, $250k, $1M, or $5M) to boost subscription growth rates by 50% per $1M spent weekly.
  * Churn Multipliers: Users demand a robust library; having 0 titles multiplies churn by 999x (halting growth), while <3 titles gives 2.0x churn and <5 titles gives 1.5x churn.
- Content Library & Decay: Fill your library with self-produced original creations (50% content score bonus to attract subscribers) or license content catalogs from other studios. Library entries lose freshness by 0.5% weekly (min 0.2), requiring constant additions of new titles.
- Catalog Maintenance Fees: Balanced platform economy with weekly catalog maintenance fees (0.5% of rival studio valuation) for active catalog licensing deals.
- Co-Production Pitching: Pitch movie, TV series, animated show, or comic book adaptations of your Tier 3+ franchises to external movie studios. Share the budgets (40-50% player contribution) and split the revenues.
- Theatrical & Streaming Deals: Negotiate box office deals for feature films or streaming licensing packages.
- Milestones & Prestige: Reach milestone subscriber counts (10k, 100k, 1M, 10M, and 50M+) to unlock Prestige levels (up to Tier 5) and increase platform renown.

6. Advanced Settings (Experimental)
- Disable Engine Overload Penalty: Toggle the engine overload limit to freely stack features without scoring penalties, and automatically clean 80% of project bugs.
- Baseline Calibration: An admin calibration tool to reset design and technology baselines, ensuring your game remains balanced regardless of playstyle.
